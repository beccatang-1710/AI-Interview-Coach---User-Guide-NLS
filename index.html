Welcome to your AI Interview Coach! This Gem is specifically customised to conduct a professional mock interview and provide structured feedback to prepare you for the coming EAII job interview task. Follow the steps below to set up and practise on your mobile phone.

欢迎使用您的 AI 面试教练！本应用专门定制用于进行专业的模拟面试，并提供结构化的反馈，帮助您为即将到来的 EAII 求职面试任务做好准备。请按照以下步骤在您的手机上设置并练习。

1📱 Install the Gemini App on Your Device

📱 在您的设备上安装 Gemini 应用

To use the live voice conversation features, you must use the official mobile app for your specific phone:

要使用实时语音对话功能，您必须使用适用于您手机的官方移动应用：

🍏 iPhone Users

🍏 iPhone 用户

  1. Open the App Store and search for Google Gemini.
    打开 App Store 并搜索 Google Gemini。
  2. Download the standalone Google Gemini app (do not use the standard Google search app).
    下载独立的 Google Gemini 应用（不要使用标准的 Google 搜索应用）。
  3. Sign in using your Google account.
    使用您的 Google 账户登录。
🤖 Android Users

🤖 Android 用户

  1. Open the Google Play Store and download the Google Gemini app.
    打开 Google Play 商店并下载 Google Gemini 应用。
  2. Sign in using your Google account.
    使用您的 Google 账户登录。

2🔗 Add the Coach to Your Account

🔗 将教练添加到您的账户

On your mobile phone, click the link below to open your coach:

在您的手机上，点击以下链接打开您的教练：

👉 Open 4102_EAII Job Interview Coach

👉 打开 4102_EAII 求职面试教练

The link will open the Coach page in your phone's web browser.

该链接将在您手机的网络浏览器中打开教练页面。

Crucial Action: Type "Good morning" and send this first message.

关键操作：输入 "Good morning" 并发送此第一条消息。

⚠️ Note: Sending that first message is what permanently "saves" the shared Coach to your Gemini account. Once you do this, it will officially show up inside your mobile app!

⚠️ 注意：发送该第一条消息将把共享的教练永久"保存"到您的 Gemini 账户。一旦您这样做，它将正式显示在您的移动应用中！

4📋 Understand the Interview Flow & Question Bank

📋 了解面试流程和问题库

This Coach follows a specific script and behavioural design. Here is exactly how your session will flow:

本教练遵循特定的脚本和行为设计。以下是您的会话流程：

⚙️ The Interview Process

⚙️ 面试流程

The Trigger

触发

The coach will wait until you say "Good morning" to begin.

教练将等待您说 "Good morning" 才开始。

The Opening

开场

It will then ask: "Could you please introduce yourself in 2 minutes?"

然后它会问："请用两分钟介绍一下自己好吗？"

The Core Interview

核心面试

After your introduction, it will randomly select and ask 3 to 5 questions total per session from the question bank below, one at a time.

在您自我介绍后，它将从下方的问题库中随机选择并每次提出 3 到 5 个问题，一次一个。

The Goal

目标

The coach acts as a professional job interviewer and will provide concise, structured feedback on your performance.

教练扮演专业求职面试官的角色，并将对您的表现提供简洁、结构化的反馈。

🗂️ Potential Questions You Will Face

🗂️ 您将面对的潜在问题

Category / 类别Sample Interview Questions / 面试问题示例
Career Development / Personality Assessment
职业发展 / 个性评估
  • What are your goals for the next 5/10 years? How do you plan to achieve those goals?
    您未来 5/10 年的目标是什么？您计划如何实现这些目标？

  • What is your personal growth and development plan?
    您的个人成长和发展计划是什么？
  • Would you consider taking any training courses related to this field/job?
    您会考虑参加与该领域/工作相关的培训课程吗？
  • What would you like to study in the near future?
    您近期想学习什么？
  • What are your strengths/weaknesses?
    您的优点/缺点是什么？
  • What three words would you use to describe yourself?
    您会用哪三个词来形容自己？
  • What do you do in your spare time?
    您空闲时间做什么？
Technical / Knowledge-based
技术 / 知识类
  • How are your studies at IVE related to this job?
    您在 IVE 的学习与这份工作有何关联？
  • What have you learnt at IVE to prepare you for this job?
    您在 IVE 学到了什么来为这份工作做准备？
  • What is your favourite subject at IVE? What have you learnt from it?
    您在 IVE 最喜欢的科目是什么？您从中学到了什么？

能力 / 情境类

    描述一个困难的工作情况/项目，并解释您是如何克服的。
  • How would you deal with difficult customers/patients/colleagues?
    您将如何应对难缠的客户/患者/同事？

    描述一次您在压力下表现良好的经历。您是如何处理该情况的？

    您能举一个您独立工作能力的例子吗？
  • Do you work better as a team member or alone? Give an example.
    您作为团队成员还是单独工作效果更好？请举例说明。
